New Delhi – January 6th-9th – Las Vegas Convention Center once again witnessed the spectacular 2017 International Consumer Electronics Show, which attracts more than 3,600 exhibitors from over 150 countries and regions worldwide and is covered in real-time by roughly 100 major foreign media agencies. As the global leader in household appliances and consumer electronics, Haier, in addition to exhibiting a range of innovative smart products, drew the attention of a fascinated crowd by making the first overseas debut of UHome-OS, the ecosystem operating system specially developed and customized for smart homes in the age of the Internet of Things.
