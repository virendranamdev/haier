BEIJING, China–December 9th, 2016 –Beijing was witness to the launch of Casarte’snewest electric wine cooler, powered by Haier’s new compressor-free thin-film solid-state refrigeration technology. This disruptive refrigeration technology, known as the “Tesla ofhomeappliances”, ushers in an era of wine coolers that are compressor-free, silent-running, vibration-less, and that are able to hold a constant temperature. The sector is set for a real breakthrough as a result of Haier’s innovation.
