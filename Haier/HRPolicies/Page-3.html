MOSCOW, Russia – MVIDEO, Russia’s largest chain of retail enterprises engaged in household appliances, announced the results of its annual rankings with Haier refrigerators at the top of the list. By virtue of Haier’s excellent reputation and high sales volume among consumers, the company’s Italian-style 3-door refrigerators took the award of “Best Product Design of the Year”. At the same time, Haier become the primary mid- and high-end refrigerator brand promoted by MVIDEO, “the household appliances display window of Russia”. In the past three months, sales have quadrupled in the Russian market according to data compiled by Haier Refrigerator.
