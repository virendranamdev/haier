Insurance benefit to employees. Employees' Deposit Linked Insurance scheme provides for a lump sum payment to the insured's nominated beneficiary in the event of death due to natural causes, illness or accident. Nowadays, most organisations offer the facility of provident fund (PF).

2.5      Employee Discounts on Product

All confirmed employees of the company are eligible for this benefit.

The benefit would be 25% of discount on DP or the Floor Price on any of the company’s products which the employee may wish to purchase for his/her personal use. The discount limit will be approved by Product Group Heads (PGH).

An employee may avail these benefit not more than two products from each category in a calendar year.

 

Procedure of Claiming Benefit

a)     Eligible employee will fill the Employee Discount Form (Ref Annexure 1) mentioning product details, get the same approved by respective Product head and submit the same to HR Operations.

b)    Employee will make the expected payment as specified by Product Head and mention the payment details on the form.

c)     HR Operations will verify the details of the form and make entry in the employee discount database and hand over the form back to the employee on the same day of receipt of the form.

d)    Employee will get the form approved by HR Head and submit the same to head for his/ her approval and submit this fully approved form to SOD on the same day.

e)     SOD will be responsible to generate SOD reference number and inform the concerned Branch Commercial Manager/ HO billing and HO Commercial within 24 working hours of receipt of the approved form.

f)      BCM/ HO Billing will process the order based on SOD reference number and will be responsible to send the information to HO Commercial for expected price releasing within 24 working hours of receipt of SOD Reference number subject to availability of stock.

g)    HO commercial will confirm the expected price releasing to BCM/ HO Billing within 24 working hours of receipt of information from BCM/ HO Billing.

h)     BCM/HO Billing is responsible to send the request for Out Bound Delivery (OBD) releasing to credit control department on the same day of receipt of expected price releasing.

i)      Credit control is responsible to confirm the collection against the order, releases the OBD and passes the info to BCM/HO Billing within 24 working hours.

j)      BCM/HO Billing will generate invoice on receipt of OBD to help employee getting the product.

k)     BCM is responsible to forward the invoice details to the employee as well as to the BLM for delivery of product on the address mentioned in the form.

 

Respective BLM is responsible for delivery of the product within 5 days of the invoice generation.

An eligible employee may claim this benefit within maximum of 30 days before or after date of marriage. Intimation of marriage in a form of wedding invitation card is mandatory to be submitted to HR Operations while claiming this benefit.

2.7      Soft Loan for Employees

 

Eligible Criteria

All HIL confirmed employees whoever completed 2 Year of service with Haier Appliances.

Conditions

Employee can claim the Advance Soft Loan based on below reasons only

                           I.        Medical Emergency (Employee or Dependents)

                          II.        Purchase or Renovation of Property

                         III.        Children Education Support (Admission Purpose)

                        IV.        Self-Education

                         V.        Wedding Assistance

                        VI.        Vehicle Purchase

 

General Guidelines

2.7.1       All 2 year old (from joining date) confirmed employees are eligible to avail this benefit.

2.7.2       A request for a soft loan should only be made in cases as per reasons mentioned in clause 2.2.1.

2.7.3       Approval to any of such requests is purely an organizational discretion.

2.7.4       Employee is eligible to request for the Soft Loan amount as per the table mentioned below in Entitlement section. (Refer Section No. 5)

2.7.5       Employee shall request for such advance maximum once in a calendar Year.

2.7.6       The recovery will start from the current month salary if the advance is disbursed before 10th of the month and recovery will start from the next month if the advance amount is disbursed after 10th of the month.

2.7.7       The amount will be recovered from the Salary in minimum 6 or maximum 8 equal installments, as chosen by employee and approved by HR HOD.

2.7.8       In case employee leaves before the completion of the scheduled repayment tenure (as approved at the time of loan disbursement), there shall be Interest charged to the total approved loan amount @ 11% per annum.

 

2.7.9       All approved requests (approved by immediate manager and HOD) received during the month will be consolidated by HR, and final approval would be given in first week of next month based on :

2.7.9.1       Reason/ purpose stated by employee

2.7.9.2       Priority to junior grade employees

2.7.9.3       Priority to be given for medical exigency

2.7.9.4       Overall Fund availability for disbursement of soft loan.

2.7.10     After final approval by HR HOD and president, (Authorization matrix in 8.1) Finance will transfer the amount to the Employee’s account within 5 working days.

2.7.11     Upon receipt of Loan amount, Employee needs to furnish acknowledgement or proof of money’s usage within 7 working days of loan disbursement. Failing which the complete amount will be deducted from monthly salary.

2.7.12     In case of deviation, approval needs to be taken from HR HOD and MD.

 

Procedure

 

2.7.13     Eligible employee will fill the Advance Soft Loan (Ref Annexure 1) mentioning problem details and to get the same approved by Immediate Manager & HOD before submit to HR Operations.

2.7.14     Employee must mention the Reason for the request in the form.

2.7.15     HR Operations will verify the details of the form and take the approval of the HR HOD & President of the HIL.

Risk cover:

§  In case any employee who is eligible as per clause 3, all such employees need to furnish a blank Cheque duly signed of the salary account maintained with Haier.

§  HR operations would maintain record of all approved request forms, loans approved and disbursed along with physical blank Cheque received from employees.

§  On settlement of soft loan (all installments paid), the blank Cheque would be returned to employee by HR operations.

·         If the expatriate finish his or her calling balance before the current month end, he or she will recharge the same at their own expense and Haier India will not be liable for same.

Process of Getting a Number/Sim Card

·         All expatriate who require a phone connection /SIM card in India will have to register their name and details with the Administration department of HIL and collect the SIM request form (Annexure I).

·         The SIM request form should be submitted to Administration duly signed by the CFO and HOD Strategy.

·         All applications will be required to provide 2 Passport Size Photographs & a copy of Passport to the administration department.

·         Administration department will contact the service provider and facilitate application of SIM and all expats have to fill in the application accordingly at a time communicated by Admin.

·         As per the rules of TRAI (Telephone Regulatory Authority of India), the application will, go for verification and once the same is cleared the SIM will be activated. The process takes 5 working days.

PROCESS OF SURRENDERING A NUMBER/SIM CARD

·         All expat will have to inform one week in advance about their departure from India and the last date to surrender/disconnect the connection, to the Administration department via mail.

·         Once the date is confirmed the connection will be terminated on the specified date, however in case of extension of stay, the same should be communicated in advance.

 

PROCESS FOR USE OF THE SAME NUMBER ON SUBSEQUENT VISITS

Certain expats may want to retain the same number on their subsequent visits to India. In such cases they need to clearly specify their requirement in writing to the Administration department of HIL.

 

·         In this case the phone will be kept in suspended mode by paying a certain amount in order to avoid unnecessary rentals.

·         During the next visit a request of activation of the same has to be sent to the Administration department of HIL, who will get the same activated.
