Introducing Haier

Dear Colleagues,
In our continuous endeavor to become a great place to work, we proudly announce launch of Haier- Our Communication, Engagement and Rewards Platform.
Haier is a mobile-based platformhelpingus roll-out important communication, HR and Engagement Programs. This will also enable you to access important updates, information and organizational news on the go.
An exciting feature of Haier is that it will enable you to get Corporate Perks/ Discounts at participating merchants. Participating merchants include Hospitals, Play-Schools, Fitness Centres, Salons, Spa, Dining, Online Stores and many more
We are launching Haier in collaboration with our technical partner- Benepik. You will shortly be informed about the login process. Please download Haier App on your Android/ iOS Phone.
Should you have any queries, please do not hesitate to contact me.
